Conclusion

February 2026 recorded a significant drop in overall traffic across all main acquisition channels. Users declined by 42% (329 vs 568), sessions by 39% (439 vs 721), and both organic and direct traffic fell substantially. No SEO updates to the site were made during the month. LinkedIn posts and one newsletter were published, contributing to a slight increase in organic social traffic (10 vs 8 sessions).

From an SEO perspective, the site continues to strengthen its structural visibility. Impressions reached 21,779 (+7%), the highest level in recent months. Average position improved further to 13 (vs 15 in January) — confirming a positive long-term ranking trajectory. However, CTR declined to 1.0%, widening the gap between visibility and traffic conversion.

Despite the volume drop, engagement metrics improved: bounce rate decreased to 71.07% (-4.10pp), engagement rate increased to 28.93% (+4.10pp), and average session duration gained 10 seconds. This confirms the recurring pattern in this technical B2B environment — fewer but more qualified visitors interact more deeply with the site content.

Keyword analysis shows that highly specific, intent-driven queries continue to perform efficiently. The branded query "oneida recherche services" achieves a 14.3% CTR at position 1.9. The generic term "hermeticity" (697 impressions, 0 clicks) remains a persistent conversion gap despite strong visibility.

Tool pages remain the strongest organic assets. The Moisture Calculator and Conversions d'humidité pages continue to consolidate their leadership and generate the majority of organic clicks.

Actions

  1. Publish the proposed content on /outils/conversions-dhumidite/ page (French and English versions)
  2. Rework the structure of the /tests-dhermeticite/hshld/ page to improve its average position and increase its CTR
  3. Optimize titles and meta descriptions on high-impression pages (hermeticity, gross leak test) to close the visibility-to-click gap
  4. Monitor March traffic to determine whether the February decline is seasonal or structural
  5. Prioritize PDF conversion into structured HTML pages — starting with Variable_leak_rate_phenomena_in_glass_to_metal_seals.pdf
