Conclusion

November 2025 shows a generally positive continuation of the SEO trends observed in recent months. Overall visibility improved, with a notable increase in impressions and organic sessions. Humidity conversions and moisture calculation tools remain the strongest organic entry points.

User acquisition is stable, but engagement remains fragile. This reinforces the need to improve landing-page relevance, UX clarity and internal linking, especially on high-impression technical pages.

Organic and direct channels continue to drive most of the traffic, and the email campaign likely contributed to higher direct activity.

Looking Ahead

Success in Q1 2026 depends on executing three interconnected priorities: engagement optimization through enhanced content depth, CTR improvement via strategic refinement of titles and meta descriptions, and content modernization starting with the humidity conversion page update.

These initiatives, combined with continued monitoring of algorithm changes and competitive landscape shifts, position orslabs.fr for sustained organic growth and improved user engagement throughout 2026.
