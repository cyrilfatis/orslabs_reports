Conclusion

December 2025 marks a seasonal slowdown in traffic and acquisition, largely driven by reduced demand during the festive period and the absence of a newsletter campaign (which had boosted November's direct traffic). Organic and direct traffic declined significantly, while referral and paid channels remained marginal.

Major Milestone: LinkedIn Success

The most significant positive development of December is the emergence of organic social traffic from LinkedIn. For the first time, the LinkedIn posts and articles published during the month generated 18 new sessions — up from 0 in all previous months. This represents a breakthrough in B2B social media visibility for ORS Labs.

This channel demonstrates high potential for reaching technical decision-makers in the hermeticity and packaging testing industry. Continuing and expanding LinkedIn content strategy should be a key priority for 2026.

From an SEO perspective, visibility continued to grow with impressions up 7%, but performance efficiency declined. The current challenge is conversion at the SERP level, in addition to visibility.

On the positive side, engagement metrics improved noticeably:

  • Bounce rate decreased from 75.93% to 70.50%
  • Session duration increased from 1:12 to 1:30
  • Engagement rate rose from 24.05% to 29.50%

This indicates that traffic quality improved — visitors are more engaged even if less numerous.

Looking Ahead

Success in Q1 2026 depends on executing four interconnected priorities:

  1. CTR optimization through strategic refinement of titles and meta descriptions on high-visibility pages
  2. Page strengthening with enhanced content depth and improved positioning
  3. LinkedIn strategy development to capitalize on December's breakthrough and establish a consistent B2B social presence
  4. Content modernization starting with the humidity conversion page update

December represents a temporary slowdown rather than a reversal. The combination of improved engagement quality, maintained SEO visibility, and the promising emergence of LinkedIn as a new acquisition channel positions orslabs.fr for sustained growth throughout 2026.
